Why this page?

A lot of interesting science happens at the edges of a main research program. This page is meant to hold projects that are worth showing but do not fit neatly into a single category: unusual binaries, exoplanet host-star problems, instrument concepts, student-driven explorations, or one-off analyses that turned into useful methods.
